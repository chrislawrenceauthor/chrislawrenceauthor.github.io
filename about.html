About

I write fiction that leans into big questions — what people do when the rules vanish, what love looks like under pressure, and how ordinary lives cope with extraordinary events.

What I write

  • Survival fiction with practical realism and emotional stakes.
  • Thrillers where consequences matter and the past has teeth.
  • Character-first stories — the plot hits harder when you care.

What I’m working on

I’m developing ongoing projects including Ross Awakens and Final Revenge. I also love building worlds with believable detail — places, routines, and the small practicalities that make a story feel real.
